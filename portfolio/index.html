Christian Cintora Portfolio


These works were created between 2019 and 2021 over the course of working towards becoming a better atists,

My artwork was created through many different attempts to experiment with different forms of art. For example, with the glitch project, there were many different attempts some that didn’t even make it to the final cut, as we made 27 different images I worked with using 12 of them. Some worked really well to show progression, and others were showing how to use different forms glitching the image. I really enjoyed making this piece as unlike my other pieces, I had a small amount of control in making glitches “properly” which made it fun to find out what did what and how. I found out there were specific changes that allowed me to glitch certain things, and I found out the work speaks for itself. The next pieces were the GIF project, and unlike Glitch, I enjoyed this one because I had the most control over these pieces. The time I spent trying to make them, and working late into tonight while I hated it, I can’t help but enjoy my time spent doing that. While the second project was honestly not as polished as the first one, the Rain droplet was by far my favorite one yet.
